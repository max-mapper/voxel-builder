Welcome to Voxel Critter Creator

Create your own critter!

Use your mouse to place blocks. When you are done designing your critter you can share it with the world.

Controls

click + drag to adjust angle.

click to place a voxel.

shift to delete a voxel.

alt to bulk edit voxels.

1-5 to change color.

+/- or scroll to zoom.

drop an creature .png image to load it in

Animation

Turn on Animation to optionally create a multi-frame voxel critter.

View the demo animation.
